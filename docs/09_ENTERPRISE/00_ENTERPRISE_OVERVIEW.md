# Purpose

The Enterprise Platform transforms AIVE from a research application into organizational infrastructure.

Organizations should not merely use AIVE.

They should build their institutional intelligence on top of it.

---

# Philosophy

Organizations do not need another chatbot.

Organizations need a continuously evolving intelligence platform.

The Enterprise Platform preserves organizational knowledge, accelerates discovery and enables evidence-based decision making across the entire institution.

---

# Enterprise Objectives

AIVE should help organizations:

Preserve institutional knowledge.

Unify fragmented information.

Accelerate research.

Improve collaboration.

Protect intellectual property.

Support evidence-driven decisions.

Reduce duplicated work.

Create organizational memory.

Accelerate innovation.

Enable continuous learning.
