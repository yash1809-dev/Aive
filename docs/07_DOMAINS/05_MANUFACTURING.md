# Purpose

Manufacturing transforms scientific discoveries into products that impact society.

Innovation within manufacturing determines product quality, scalability, sustainability and commercial success.

The Manufacturing Intelligence Pack enables AIVE to discover opportunities throughout industrial production systems.
