# Purpose

Battery technology is one of the most strategically important scientific fields of the twenty-first century.

Advances in batteries influence transportation, renewable energy, consumer electronics, aerospace and national infrastructure.

The Battery Research Intelligence Pack enables AIVE to discover scientifically defensible opportunities across the entire battery lifecycle.
