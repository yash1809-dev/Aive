# Purpose

Material Science sits at the intersection of chemistry, physics, engineering and manufacturing.

Discoveries often arise from understanding relationships between atomic structure, processing techniques, material properties and industrial applications.

The Material Science Intelligence Pack enables AIVE to discover these relationships while maintaining complete scientific traceability.

---

# Philosophy

Materials are not isolated substances.

Every material exists within a network of:

Composition

Structure

Processing

Properties

Performance

Application

Discovery requires reasoning across all six dimensions simultaneously.
