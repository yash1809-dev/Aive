# Purpose

Education is humanity's primary mechanism for creating, transferring and preserving knowledge.

The Education Intelligence Pack enables AIVE to support researchers, educators and institutions by discovering opportunities that improve learning, teaching and educational systems.

---

# Philosophy

Education should be:

Evidence-Based

Inclusive

Personalized

Transparent

Measurable

Continuously Improving

AIVE should augment educators rather than replace them.

Learning remains fundamentally human.
