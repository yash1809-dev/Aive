# Purpose

Aerospace engineering represents one of the most knowledge-intensive industries in the world.

Every innovation depends upon integrating physics, materials science, manufacturing, control systems, simulation and operational experience.

The Aerospace Intelligence Pack enables AIVE to reason across this complete engineering ecosystem.
