# Purpose

Climate science is inherently interdisciplinary.

Understanding climate requires integrating atmospheric science, ecology, engineering, economics, energy systems and public policy.

The Climate Science Intelligence Pack enables AIVE to continuously discover evidence-supported opportunities across this interconnected ecosystem.

---

# Philosophy

Climate intelligence should always remain:

Evidence-based

Transparent

Explainable

Interdisciplinary

Long-term

Actionable

AIVE supports informed climate decisions rather than replacing scientific expertise.
