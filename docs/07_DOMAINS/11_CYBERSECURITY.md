# Purpose

Cybersecurity is a continuously evolving knowledge domain.

Every day new attacks, vulnerabilities, defensive techniques and threat actors emerge.

The Cybersecurity Intelligence Pack enables AIVE to continuously organize, analyze and discover security intelligence before knowledge becomes obsolete.

---

# Philosophy

Cybersecurity decisions must prioritize:

Evidence

Verification

Explainability

Continuous Monitoring

Responsible Disclosure

Human Oversight

AIVE assists defenders by accelerating understanding.

Human experts remain responsible for operational security decisions.
