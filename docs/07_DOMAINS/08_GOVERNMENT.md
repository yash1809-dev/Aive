# Purpose

Governments generate enormous amounts of knowledge.

Policies.

Research.

Regulations.

Budgets.

Infrastructure.

Public Services.

Historical Records.

The Government Intelligence Pack enables AIVE to transform fragmented institutional knowledge into actionable intelligence.

---

# Philosophy

Government decisions must be:

Evidence-Based

Transparent

Auditable

Explainable

Inclusive

Long-Term

AIVE supports public decision making by organizing, validating and connecting knowledge.

It never replaces democratic processes or human accountability.

---

# Discovery Objectives

AIVE should help governments:

Evaluate public policies.

Identify policy gaps.

Predict policy outcomes.

Improve public services.

Optimize infrastructure planning.

Support disaster preparedness.

Improve budget allocation.

Detect regulatory conflicts.

Accelerate evidence-based legislation.

Improve interdepartmental collaboration.
