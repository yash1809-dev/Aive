# Purpose

Finance determines which discoveries become products.

Scientific innovation without financial viability rarely reaches society.

The Financial Intelligence Pack enables AIVE to connect research discoveries with commercial and economic reality.

---

# Philosophy

Financial reasoning should never rely on speculation alone.

Every investment recommendation must remain explainable.

Every conclusion should be supported by transparent evidence.

AIVE assists decision-making rather than replacing professional financial judgment.

---

# Discovery Objectives

AIVE should help organizations:

Evaluate investment opportunities.

Identify emerging markets.

Estimate commercialization potential.

Analyze financial risks.

Detect market trends.

Optimize capital allocation.

Support strategic planning.

Improve portfolio decisions.

Discover startup opportunities.

Identify technology investment gaps.
