# Purpose

Drug discovery is one of the most complex scientific activities performed by humanity.

It combines biology, chemistry, medicine, engineering, statistics, artificial intelligence and clinical science.

The Drug Discovery Intelligence Pack provides specialized reasoning capabilities allowing AIVE to support every stage of pharmaceutical research.

---

# Philosophy

Drug discovery is not idea generation.

Drug discovery is evidence-guided hypothesis refinement.

Every proposed opportunity must remain scientifically explainable.

Every conclusion must preserve complete provenance.

---

# Discovery Objectives

AIVE should help researchers:

Understand disease mechanisms.

Identify promising biological targets.

Connect unrelated discoveries.

Detect missing experiments.

Generate testable hypotheses.

Identify therapeutic opportunities.

Evaluate scientific novelty.

Reduce duplicated research.

Accelerate literature understanding.

Improve scientific decision making.
