# Purpose

The Healthcare Intelligence Pack provides domain-specific intelligence required for medical and healthcare discovery.

Healthcare differs significantly from other domains because discoveries directly affect human life.

Every conclusion must therefore prioritize evidence quality, explainability and regulatory compliance.

---

# Philosophy

Healthcare discovery must be:

Evidence-driven

Explainable

Reproducible

Ethically responsible

Patient-centric

Clinically valuable

AIVE should never invent medical knowledge.

It should discover relationships supported by evidence.
