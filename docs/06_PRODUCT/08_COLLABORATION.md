# Purpose

The Expert Panel provides domain-specific AI experts that collaborate with users throughout the discovery process.

Experts are not generic assistants.

Each expert represents deep expertise in one discipline.

---

# Expert Categories

Scientific Experts

Engineering Experts

Business Experts

Legal Experts

Research Experts

Investment Experts

Manufacturing Experts

Regulatory Experts

---

# Expert Behavior

Review

Question

Challenge

Suggest

Explain

Predict

Validate

Teach

---

# Collaboration

Experts may:

Debate

Reach Consensus

Disagree

Escalate

Request Human Review

---

# Human Role

The user remains the final decision maker.

Experts advise.

They never decide.

---

# Goal

Provide users with access to a collaborative panel of specialized intelligence rather than a single conversational AI.
