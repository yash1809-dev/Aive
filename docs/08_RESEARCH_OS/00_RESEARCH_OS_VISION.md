# Purpose

The Research Operating System (Research OS) is the runtime environment where discovery happens.

Previous layers define knowledge, reasoning, intelligence and infrastructure.

The Research OS coordinates all of them into one continuously evolving research environment.

Unlike traditional software, AIVE is never idle.

Knowledge evolves.

Discoveries evolve.

Reasoning evolves.

Users collaborate.

The system continuously improves.

---

# Philosophy

Research should become a living process.

Not a collection of files.

The operating system continuously understands the workspace even when no user is interacting with it.
