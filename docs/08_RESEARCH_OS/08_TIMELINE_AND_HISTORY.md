# Purpose

Branching allows multiple research directions to evolve independently without affecting the primary knowledge base.

Merging combines validated knowledge back into shared understanding.

---

# Philosophy

Ideas should branch safely.

Knowledge should merge carefully.

Understanding should continuously improve.
