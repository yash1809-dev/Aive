# Purpose

Every discovery inside AIVE progresses through a structured lifecycle.

This lifecycle ensures that discoveries mature through evidence, criticism and validation before becoming trusted knowledge.

---

↓

Knowledge

↓

↓

↓

↓

↓

↓

↓

↓

Opportunity

↓

↓

Outcome

↓

↓

Knowledge Evolution

---

# State Transitions

Transitions are triggered only by evidence.

Manual overrides should always remain auditable.

---

# Continuous Evolution

Accepted discoveries may later:

Improve.

Split.

Merge.

Become obsolete.

Generate new discoveries.

---

# Goal

Discovery should remain a living process rather than a one-time event.
