# Purpose

The Knowledge Engine maintains, enriches and evolves the entire knowledge ecosystem of AIVE.

Unlike traditional databases that merely store information, the Knowledge Engine continuously improves the quality, connectivity and semantic richness of Knowledge Objects.

It is responsible for ensuring that every engine operates on the highest-quality representation of reality available.

---

# Responsibilities

Construct Knowledge Objects.

Merge duplicate knowledge.

Resolve semantic conflicts.

Maintain ontology consistency.

Update relationships.

Trigger graph evolution.

Maintain provenance.

Handle versioning.

Maintain embeddings.

---

# Non-Responsibilities

The Knowledge Engine does NOT:

Generate discoveries.

Judge novelty.

Validate opportunities.

Produce reports.

Reason over discoveries.
