# Purpose

This document defines the complete end-to-end discovery pipeline inside AIVE.

The Discovery Pipeline describes how raw information eventually becomes validated discovery.

---

# Stages

## Stage 1

Information enters the system.

---

## Stage 2

Knowledge Objects are constructed.

---

## Stage 3

Graphs are updated.

---

## Stage 4

Reasoning begins.

---

## Stage 5

Discovery candidates emerge.

---

## Stage 6

Novelty Engine attempts to invalidate them.

---

## Stage 7

Critic Engine challenges assumptions.

---

## Stage 8

Validation Engine measures trustworthiness.

---

## Stage 9

Accepted discoveries become persistent knowledge.

---

## Stage 10

Learning Engine updates future reasoning.

---

# Feedback

Every stage produces feedback.

Failures improve future discovery.

Successful discoveries strengthen future reasoning.

---

# Continuous Execution

The pipeline never stops.

New information continuously re-enters Stage 1.

Discovery therefore becomes recursive.

---

# Goal

Transform humanity's expanding knowledge into continuously improving discovery.
