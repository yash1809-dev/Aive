# Purpose

The Reasoning Engine transforms structured understanding into candidate explanations, hypotheses and discoveries.

Unlike language models that primarily generate text, the Reasoning Engine operates over Knowledge Objects and semantic relationships.

---

# Responsibilities

Pattern Recognition

Relationship Analysis

Hypothesis Generation

Constraint Satisfaction

Scenario Simulation

Causal Reasoning

Cross-Domain Reasoning

Reasoning Graph Construction

---

# Non-Responsibilities

Does not validate.

Does not rank novelty.

Does not criticize.

Does not generate reports.
