# Purpose

The Discovery Graph represents the evolution of discoveries throughout the lifetime of AIVE.

Instead of treating discoveries as isolated outputs, AIVE models them as a connected ecosystem.

---

# Philosophy

Every discovery builds upon previous discoveries.

Scientific progress is cumulative.

The Discovery Graph captures this evolution.
