# Purpose

The Reasoning Graph represents how understanding evolves into discoveries.

Unlike the Evidence Graph, which tracks justification, the Reasoning Graph tracks cognition.

---

# Philosophy

Evidence explains why something may be true.

Reasoning explains how conclusions emerge.

The Reasoning Graph records every major reasoning pathway inside AIVE.

# Multiple Paths

The same discovery may emerge through multiple independent reasoning paths.

AIVE should preserve all reasoning routes rather than only the final result.

---

# Reasoning Quality

Reasoning quality depends on:

Evidence Coverage

Logical Consistency

Explainability

Novelty

Reproducibility

Expert Agreement

---

# Continuous Evolution

Reasoning paths improve over time.

Better evidence may simplify or replace older reasoning.

Historical reasoning should remain accessible.

---

# Goal

The Reasoning Graph transforms AI reasoning from hidden computation into persistent knowledge.
