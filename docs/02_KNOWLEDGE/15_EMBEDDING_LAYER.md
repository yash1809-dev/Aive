# Purpose

Embeddings provide the semantic representation of Knowledge Objects.

Unlike traditional embeddings generated directly from documents, AIVE generates embeddings from structured Knowledge Objects.

---

# Philosophy

Documents are containers.

Knowledge Objects contain meaning.

Embeddings should therefore represent meaning rather than raw text.

---

# Why Embeddings?

Embeddings enable:

Semantic Search

Similarity Detection

Clustering

Recommendation

Knowledge Retrieval

Relationship Discovery

Cross-Domain Matching

Opportunity Detection

---

# Multi-Level Embeddings

Every object may possess multiple embeddings.

Semantic Embedding

Structural Embedding

Graph Embedding

Temporal Embedding

Domain Embedding

Relationship Embedding

These embeddings together produce richer understanding.

---

# Continuous Evolution

Embeddings evolve.

When knowledge changes,

embeddings should be updated.

Embeddings are not permanent.

---

# Relationship to AI

AI agents retrieve semantically relevant Knowledge Objects through embeddings before reasoning begins.

Embeddings therefore support reasoning rather than replacing it.

---

# Goal

Embeddings provide efficient semantic access to structured knowledge throughout AIVE.
