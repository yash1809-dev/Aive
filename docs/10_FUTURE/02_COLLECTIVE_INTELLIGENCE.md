# Purpose

Human civilization has never lacked intelligence.

It has lacked coordination.

Knowledge exists.

Experts exist.

Evidence exists.

What rarely exists is the ability to combine them efficiently.

Collective Intelligence is AIVE's long-term vision for enabling humanity to think together without centralizing ownership.

---

# Philosophy

Collective Intelligence does not mean everyone shares everything.

It means organizations contribute knowledge when appropriate, retain ownership of their work and collaborate through trusted evidence.

AIVE amplifies collaboration rather than replacing independent research.

---

# Fundamental Principle

Collective Intelligence emerges when independent knowledge systems communicate through transparent evidence rather than isolated documents.

Organizations remain autonomous.

Knowledge becomes interoperable.
