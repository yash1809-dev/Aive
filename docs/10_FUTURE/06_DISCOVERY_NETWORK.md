# Purpose

Knowledge already exists everywhere.

Discovery does not.

Organizations continuously solve similar problems without realizing someone else has already solved a related challenge.

The Discovery Network exists to connect discoveries rather than organizations.

---

# Philosophy

The network is not built around data.

It is built around validated understanding.

Organizations remain independent.

Knowledge remains owned.

Discovery becomes collaborative.

---

# Why It Exists

Today's research ecosystem is fragmented.

Universities.

Hospitals.

Governments.

Enterprises.

Laboratories.

Startups.

Research Groups.

Each develops valuable knowledge.

Very little becomes connected.

The Discovery Network bridges these isolated intelligence systems.
