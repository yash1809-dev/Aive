# Purpose

Human knowledge is currently fragmented.

Scientific papers exist separately from patents.

Patents exist separately from products.

Products exist separately from operational knowledge.

Operational knowledge exists separately from scientific research.

Organizations continuously rediscover knowledge that already exists somewhere else.

The Planetary Knowledge Graph exists to reduce this fragmentation.

# Philosophy

The Planetary Knowledge Graph is NOT a database.

It is not a search engine.

It is not a central repository.

It is a continuously evolving representation of humanity's understanding.

Knowledge remains distributed.

Understanding becomes connected.

# Privacy

The Planetary Knowledge Graph is not built by exposing private information.

Organizations contribute only what they choose.

Private Knowledge Clouds remain private.

Public knowledge remains public.

Shared knowledge remains permissioned.

Ownership never disappears.
