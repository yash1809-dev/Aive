It is a strategic north star.

As technology changes, implementation may change.

# Long-Term Vision

Twenty years from now...

Organizations should no longer ask,

"Where is our information?"

They should ask,

"What does our knowledge suggest we should discover next?"

That transition defines the success of AIVE.

---

# Final Statement

This roadmap is not a prediction.

It is a commitment.

Every stage should increase humanity's ability to preserve knowledge, deepen understanding and accelerate discovery while remaining transparent, explainable and fundamentally human-centered.
