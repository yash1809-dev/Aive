# Purpose

Knowledge evolves.

Science evolves.

Industries evolve.

Organizations evolve.

AIVE must evolve alongside them.

Self-Evolving Intelligence enables the Discovery Operating System to improve continuously without abandoning transparency, evidence or human oversight.

---

# Philosophy

AIVE should not rewrite itself.

It should improve itself.

The platform evolves through learning.

Not through uncontrolled autonomy.
