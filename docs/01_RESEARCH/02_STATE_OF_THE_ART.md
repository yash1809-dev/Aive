# Purpose

This document reviews the current landscape of knowledge systems.

The objective is not to criticize existing tools.

The objective is to identify the capabilities that remain missing.

---

# Categories

Current systems generally fall into six categories.

---

## Search Engines

Strengths

- Retrieval
- Indexing
- Ranking

Weaknesses

- No persistent reasoning
- No discovery
- Limited synthesis

---

## Academic Search

Strengths

- Citation networks
- Paper discovery

Weaknesses

- Human interpretation required
- No opportunity generation

---

## Large Language Models

Strengths

- Language understanding
- Summarization
- Question answering
- Planning

Weaknesses

- Session memory
- Evidence persistence
- Structured discovery
- Novelty verification

---

## Knowledge Graphs

Strengths

- Structured relationships
- Persistent knowledge

Weaknesses

- Limited autonomous reasoning
- Manual construction
- Static representations

---

## RAG Systems

Strengths

- Better factual grounding

Weaknesses

- Retrieval-focused
- Limited long-term memory
- Minimal discovery capability

---

## Multi-Agent Systems

Strengths

- Task specialization
- Parallel reasoning

Weaknesses

- Often lack persistent structured knowledge
- Weak evidence models
- Poor discovery validation

---

# Missing Capability

Across all existing categories,

one capability consistently remains underdeveloped:

Continuous Evidence-Driven Discovery.

This capability requires:

- persistent knowledge
- structured evidence
- reasoning
- novelty verification
- criticism
- validation
- learning

No existing category fully integrates all of these components.

---

# Position of AIVE

AIVE does not attempt to replace existing systems.

Instead,

it integrates and extends them into a unified discovery platform.

Retrieval becomes one capability.

Reasoning becomes another.

Validation becomes another.

Discovery emerges from the interaction of all capabilities rather than any individual component.

---

# Conclusion

The next generation of intelligent systems will likely move beyond retrieval and generation toward continuous discovery.

AIVE is designed around this transition.
