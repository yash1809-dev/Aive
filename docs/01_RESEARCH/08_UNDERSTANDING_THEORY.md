# Purpose

This document introduces Understanding as an independent layer between Knowledge and Reasoning.

Understanding is one of the central theoretical contributions of AIVE.

---

# Why Understanding Exists

Knowledge alone does not produce discoveries.

A system may possess millions of documents yet understand nothing.

Understanding emerges only when knowledge becomes internally connected and meaningful.

---

# Information Hierarchy

Information

↓

Knowledge

↓

Understanding

↓

Reasoning

↓

Discovery

Each layer transforms the previous one.

---

# Information

Raw observations.

No interpretation.

---

# Knowledge

Structured information.

Entities.

Claims.

Evidence.

Methods.

Experiments.

---

# Understanding

Understanding emerges when relationships between knowledge become coherent.

Understanding answers:

Why?

How?

Under what conditions?

What if?

Understanding is relational rather than informational.

---

# Characteristics

Understanding possesses:

Context.

Relationships.

Hierarchy.

Causality.

Dependencies.

Abstractions.

Uncertainty.

---

# Continuous Understanding

Understanding evolves.

Every new knowledge object changes existing understanding.

Understanding should therefore remain dynamic.

---

# Why It Matters

Most AI systems stop at knowledge.

AIVE attempts to construct understanding before reasoning.

Reasoning without understanding becomes pattern matching.

Reasoning with understanding enables discovery.

---

# Goal

Understanding is the bridge that transforms structured knowledge into meaningful reasoning.

Without Understanding, Discovery Science cannot exist.
