# Purpose

This document establishes Discovery Science as the theoretical foundation of AIVE.

Discovery Science studies how new knowledge emerges from existing knowledge.

---

# Definition

Discovery Science is the systematic process of transforming structured knowledge into evidence-backed understanding through reasoning, validation and continuous learning.

Unlike Information Retrieval,

Discovery Science does not ask:

"What information exists?"

Instead it asks:

"What meaningful understanding can emerge?"

---

# Discovery Pipeline

Within AIVE,

Discovery follows this process.

```

Observation

↓

Information

↓

Knowledge Extraction

↓

Knowledge Objects

↓

Understanding

↓

Reasoning

↓

Hypothesis

↓

Novelty Verification

↓

Criticism

↓

Discovery

↓

Validation

↓

Learning

↓

Updated Knowledge

```

---

# Four Layers

## Layer 1 — Observation

Raw information.

Examples:

- papers
- patents
- experiments
- reports

---

## Layer 2 — Understanding

Objects become meaningful.

Claims.

Evidence.

Methods.

Relationships.

---

## Layer 3 — Reasoning

Understanding interacts.

Patterns emerge.

Contradictions appear.

Opportunities become visible.

---

## Layer 4 — Discovery

Validated reasoning produces new knowledge.

The discovery re-enters the knowledge ecosystem.

The cycle continues.

---

# Continuous Evolution

Discovery is not an endpoint.

Every discovery becomes new knowledge.

That knowledge enables future discoveries.

Discovery therefore forms a recursive system.

---

# Scientific Properties

A discovery should satisfy:

✓ Evidence

✓ Explainability

✓ Novelty

✓ Actionability

✓ Validation

✓ Revision Capability

---

# Discovery Is Recursive

Today's discoveries become tomorrow's observations.

Therefore AIVE should continuously improve through accumulated understanding.

---

# Conclusion

Discovery Science provides the theoretical framework upon which every engine inside AIVE is constructed.

Without Discovery Science,

AIVE becomes another AI application.

With Discovery Science,

AIVE becomes a continuously evolving discovery platform.
