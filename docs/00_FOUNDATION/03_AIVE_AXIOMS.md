# Purpose

This document defines the immutable axioms of AIVE.

Unlike principles, axioms are assumed to be fundamentally true within the AIVE ecosystem.

Everything else is derived from these statements.

# Axiom 1

Knowledge exists independently of understanding.

The existence of information does not imply the existence of discovery.

---

# Axiom 2

Discovery is created by relationships.

Individual facts rarely create breakthroughs.

Relationships between facts create discoveries.

---

# Axiom 3

Evidence is more valuable than confidence.

Confidence is only meaningful when supported by evidence.

---

# Axiom 4

Every discovery begins as uncertainty.

The purpose of reasoning is to reduce uncertainty rather than eliminate it completely.

---

# Axiom 5

Contradictions are opportunities.

Whenever reliable evidence disagrees, there exists an opportunity for deeper understanding.

---

# Axiom 6

Knowledge continuously evolves.

No conclusion is permanent.

Every conclusion remains revisable.

---

# Axiom 7

Every piece of knowledge has context.

Removing context reduces truthfulness.

---

# Axiom 8

Discovery requires criticism.

Ideas become stronger only after surviving systematic attack.

---

# Axiom 9

Memory compounds intelligence.

A system that forgets cannot continuously improve.

---

# Axiom 10

Artificial Intelligence augments human intelligence.

It does not replace human purpose, ethics or responsibility.

---

# Axiom 11

Every important output should be explainable.

An unexplained answer cannot become trusted knowledge.

---

# Axiom 12

Reasoning is more valuable than generation.

Generation creates possibilities.

Reasoning identifies which possibilities deserve attention.

---

# Axiom 13

Knowledge should remain structured.

Structure enables reasoning.

Unstructured information limits discovery.

---

# Axiom 14

The objective of AIVE is discovery.

Everything else exists only to support that objective.
