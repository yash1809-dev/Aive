# Purpose

This document answers the most common conceptual questions regarding AIVE.

It serves as the first reference for new contributors before reading the technical documentation.

---

# Why does AIVE exist?

Humanity generates knowledge faster than humans can synthesize it.

AIVE exists to bridge that gap by continuously transforming evidence into discoveries.

---

# Is AIVE another chatbot?

No.

Conversation is only one possible interface.

AIVE is fundamentally a discovery engine.

---

# Is AIVE another RAG system?

No.

Retrieval-Augmented Generation retrieves information.

AIVE reasons over structured knowledge to produce discoveries.

Retrieval is only one component.

---

# Does AIVE replace researchers?

No.

AIVE augments human reasoning.

Researchers remain responsible for defining objectives, interpreting significance and validating discoveries.

---

# Why not use ChatGPT?

ChatGPT excels at conversation and language generation.

AIVE focuses on persistent structured knowledge, evidence-driven reasoning, novelty verification and continuous discovery.

---

# Why use multiple agents?

Discovery requires multiple perspectives.

Independent reasoning followed by structured consensus produces stronger discoveries than isolated reasoning.

---

# Why does AIVE emphasize evidence?

Trust should emerge from transparent evidence rather than fluent language.

Evidence allows discoveries to be audited, challenged and improved.

---

# What is an opportunity?

An evidence-backed, actionable discovery that has measurable scientific, technological or commercial potential.

---

# Can AIVE invent new facts?

No.

AIVE never invents evidence.

It discovers previously hidden relationships between existing evidence.

---

# Why is novelty verification necessary?

Without novelty verification, generated ideas may simply repeat existing knowledge.

AIVE attempts to disprove novelty before accepting a discovery.

---

# Why is criticism built into the system?

Scientific progress depends on criticism.

Every important discovery should survive systematic challenge before being accepted.

---

# Why does AIVE maintain memory?

Discovery compounds over time.

Persistent knowledge allows future reasoning to build upon previous understanding rather than repeatedly starting from scratch.

---

# Can AIVE work outside scientific research?

Yes.

The discovery engine is domain-independent.

Applications include research, pharmaceuticals, startups, manufacturing, enterprise R&D, government, education and many other knowledge-intensive fields.

---

# What makes AIVE different?

Most AI systems answer questions.

AIVE continuously discovers evidence-backed opportunities and relationships across structured knowledge.

Its objective is not conversation.

Its objective is discovery.
