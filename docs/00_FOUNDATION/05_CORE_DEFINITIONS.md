# Purpose

This document establishes the canonical definitions used throughout AIVE.

Every technical document should reference these definitions instead of redefining terminology.

---

# Knowledge

Structured understanding extracted from observations, evidence and experience.

---

# Information

Raw data that has not yet been interpreted.

---

# Evidence

Verifiable support for or against a claim.

---

# Claim

A statement that can be supported or contradicted using evidence.

---

# Observation

A recorded fact without interpretation.

---

# Discovery

An evidence-supported relationship that produces meaningful new understanding.

---

# Opportunity

An actionable discovery with measurable potential value.

---

# Hypothesis

A proposed explanation awaiting validation.

---

# Contradiction

Two or more evidence-supported claims that cannot simultaneously remain true without further explanation.

---

# Novelty

The degree to which a discovery differs from existing validated knowledge.

---

# Reasoning

The structured process of transforming knowledge into discoveries.

---

# Knowledge Object

A persistent entity representing structured knowledge together with metadata, evidence and relationships.

---

# Knowledge Graph

The structured network connecting knowledge objects.

---

# Evidence Graph

The structured network connecting evidence, claims and discoveries.

---

# Agent

An autonomous reasoning component responsible for a specialized cognitive task.

---

# Orchestrator

The coordination layer responsible for managing agents and constructing coherent reasoning workflows.

---

# Validation

The process of determining whether a discovery remains sufficiently supported by evidence.

---

# Discovery Engine

The collection of systems responsible for producing evidence-backed discoveries.

---

# Confidence

A probabilistic estimate of belief based upon available evidence.

---

# Uncertainty

The quantified absence or incompleteness of evidence.

---

# Intelligence

The ability to transform knowledge into useful understanding through reasoning.

---

# AIVE

An Evidence-Driven Discovery Engine designed to continuously synthesize structured knowledge into trustworthy discoveries.
