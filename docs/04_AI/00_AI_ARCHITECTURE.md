# Purpose

This document defines the Artificial Intelligence architecture of AIVE.

Unlike conventional AI systems centered around a single Large Language Model, AIVE is built as a distributed intelligence platform composed of specialized models, autonomous agents and cognitive services working together toward discovery.

The AI Layer does not define knowledge.

The AI Layer executes cognitive capabilities defined by the Engine Layer.

# Philosophy

Intelligence should be specialized.

No single model should perform every task.

Different models possess different strengths.

AIVE therefore treats models as specialists rather than universal intelligences.

---

# Design Principles

Specialization over generalization.

Cooperation over isolation.

Reasoning over generation.

Evidence over opinion.

Persistent intelligence over conversational intelligence.
